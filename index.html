The methodology

The audit is powered by Context Infrastructure: the layer of source material, proof, structure, and buyer language that makes expertise usable by people and machines.

1. What buyers see

Website, service pages, LinkedIn profile, newsletter, case studies, public proof, CTAs, and visible credibility signals.

2. What AI systems can understand

Public source material, entity clarity, category language, structured explanations, methodology pages, and retrievable proof.

3. What proof supports your claims

Testimonials, case studies, examples, screenshots, outcomes, methods, client language, and evidence attached to commercial claims.

4. What asset should be built next

Authority page, proof page, comparison page, report, newsletter sequence, founder POV asset, lead magnet, service page, or source hub.
